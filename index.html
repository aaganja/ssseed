Welcome

SSS produces OP seeds (Cucumber, Faba Beans, Broad Beans, Mustard Greens, etc) through community based seed production model. Sarba Shrestha Seeds is working with the aim to develop improved varieties in vegetables in Nepal itself.

About SSSeeds

SSSeeds

Sarba Shrestha Seeds Pvt. Ltd., established in 2015, an emerging seed company, is working its part to do a healthy seed business with its motto ‘Seeds for Nepal, Seeds from Nepal’.

Sarba Shrestha Seeds is working based on a value chain model to make sure every actor in the chain gets benefited. Sarba Shrestha Seeds supplies its seed either through agro dealers or directly to the farmers. Sarba Shrestha Seeds expects that R & D will let the company stand out among all in Nepal by providing quality seeds to every farmer.

Sarba Shrestha Seeds will stand out among all in Nepal by providing quality seeds to every farmer.

Services

Service 1
